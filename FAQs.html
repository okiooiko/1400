FAQs


Is there a car park at the park?

Yes, there is an free open-air car park available for Springbrook National Park guests.

Is there internet access at the park?

Yes, Wi-Fi is currently available to guests in some locations throughout the park and it's completely free, but some locations have poor signale.

Is it possible to camp in the park?

Yes, we have a dedicated area for camping. But when you leave, please clean up the rubbish in time and protect the natural environment. Camping is not permitted anywhere in Springbrook National Park except at The Settlement camping area. For specific instructions, please refer to: looking after informantion

Here is the park camping map: Settlement camp ground map

Are there facilities for the disabled in the park?

Yes, some areas are wheelchair accessible. You can check the park map for details. If you need help, please call us: 0712345678
